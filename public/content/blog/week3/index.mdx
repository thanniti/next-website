# What is Engineering Drawing?
<Figure image="projection2.png" width="50%" imageCaption="projection"/>
Throughout the design process, engineering drawings are used to communicate design concepts and technical information to engineers and other experts. Using projection, an engineering drawing depicts a complex three-dimensional item on a two-dimensional piece of paper or computer screen.
